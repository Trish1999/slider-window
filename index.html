Summer

Summer is a vibrant season marked by long, sunny days and warm temperatures. It's a time when nature is in full bloom, with lush greenery and colorful flowers. People often enjoy outdoor activities like swimming, hiking, and picnics. Refreshing fruits like watermelon and mangoes are abundant. Summer also brings a sense of relaxation and joy, making it a favorite time for vacations and gatherings.

Monsoon

Monsoon season brings a refreshing change with its heavy rains, cooling down the summer heat. The skies often darken with clouds, creating a dramatic backdrop for the lush greenery that thrives during this time. Winter

Winter is a season of crisp, cool air and serene landscapes blanketed in snow. The days are shorter, with long, cozy nights spent by the fire. Nature slows down, and the world feels quieter and more peaceful. It's a time for warm clothing, hot beverages, and festive gatherings. Despite the chill, winter brings a unique beauty and calmness to the world.
